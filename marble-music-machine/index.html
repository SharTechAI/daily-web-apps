Marble Music Machine

Drop marbles onto musical planks and listen to the melody they create as they bounce around!

Plank tool: Click and drag to place angled bars. Each plank plays a note based on its vertical position.

Spawner tool: Click to place marble spawners that periodically drop new marbles.

Bumper tool: Click to place circular bumpers that bounce marbles with a percussive sound.

Eraser tool: Click on any object to remove it.

Keyboard: 1-4 tools, Space pause, C clear, ? help
